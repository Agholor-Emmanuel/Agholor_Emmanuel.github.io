Stock Price Prediction Using LSTM

In this project, we developed a Deep Neural Network model to predict the future prices of Apple stock using Keras Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM). LSTM networks are highly effective for sequence prediction problems because they can retain and utilize past information. This capability was crucial for our model, as historical stock prices play a vital role in forecasting future prices.

Customer Segmentation Using K-Means Clustering

In this project, we utilized customer data from a sales company to perform k-means clustering, grouping customers into four distinct clusters based on their total monthly spending and monthly gross revenue. This analysis enabled the company to tailor discounts and offer personalized gift items to different customer segments, enhancing customer satisfaction and optimizing marketing strategies.
